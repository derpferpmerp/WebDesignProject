  • Quick Facts:



    ■ Mexico is slightly over 2,000 miles in length

    ■ Mexico has two volcanoes, both of which are near the capital Mexico City

    ■ Mexico was built on a lake, and is gradually sinking every second

    ■ In Mexico, nobody gives gifts on Christmas (December 25), rather the gift giving is saved for the dates of the three wise men

    ■ In Mexico, 92.7 percent of the population speaks only Spanish
